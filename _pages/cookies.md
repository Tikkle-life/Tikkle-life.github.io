## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website or use an application. They are widely used to make websites and apps work more efficiently and provide information to the owners.

## How We Use Cookies

Tikkle uses cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

### Essential Cookies
These cookies are necessary for the App to function properly. They enable core functionality such as:
- User authentication
- Security features
- Session management

### Analytics Cookies
We use analytics cookies to understand how users interact with the App. This helps us:
- Improve App performance
- Understand which features are most popular
- Identify and fix issues

### Preference Cookies
These cookies remember your settings and preferences, such as:
- Language preferences
- Display settings
- Personalization choices

## Types of Cookies We Use

| Cookie Type | Purpose | Duration |
|------------|---------|----------|
| Session | Authentication, security | Until browser closes |
| Persistent | Preferences, analytics | Up to 1 year |
| Third-party | Analytics services | Varies |

## Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to:
- View cookies stored on your device
- Delete cookies
- Block cookies from specific sites
- Block all cookies

Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the App.

### Browser-Specific Instructions

- **Chrome**: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies
- **Firefox**: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings > Cookies and site permissions

## Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies, including:
- Analytics providers
- Error tracking services

These third parties have their own privacy policies governing the use of cookies.
